About

Connecting technical complexity
with public understanding

Ridgeline Collaborative is an independent policy research organization producing rigorous, accessible analysis of the Pacific Northwest's energy transition. We connect technical complexity with public understanding — translating the forces reshaping our electricity system into analysis that policymakers, advocates, and the public can act on.
